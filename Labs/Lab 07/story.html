My time at Hogwarts

I was sent an invitation to join Hogwarts to master wizard programming. The sorting hat assigned me to the JavaScript house. Our captain was wizard Patrick Hill who gave us a tour of the campus. Soon, I became the youngest member of the team representing my house for the Quidditchathon.

Our recent Quidditchathon was just like any other - another day of complaining about CSS, until suddenly Deidara and Sasori from Akatsuki entered on their brooms, boots in hand and started destroying our game hardware. Members of Akatsuki are rebels who are not loyal to any house and consider HTML as a programming language.

As they continued destroying precious lines of code, and talks were not helping us in resolving things we knew that it was time for us to put up a fight. All of us rushed to the great hall, geared up in shiny belts and pompoms in our hands. It was time for... A Dance Off! Surprise Surprise!

Deidara and Sasori flaunted their bhangra skills while we teamed up and decided to follow our classic cheerleading routine. Loud music and cheers from the audience filled the great hall of hidden leaf. After a lengthy battle that lasted exactly 21 days 7 hours 13 minutes 46 seconds, it was time for the goblet of fire to announce the winner.

The wildfire roared, grumbled, shrieked, and finally spat out the results! We all gathered around the floating screen. Lo and behold! The name of the winner was encrypted.

And so, we set on to our next adventure of decoding this..
